about

Hi! I’m Kush. I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin in May 2022 with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Engineering specializing in Data Science and Information Processing. Over my time at UT, I worked as an Undergraduate Research Assistant at the Socially Intelligent Machines Lab with Professor Andrea Thomaz, was a part of the Punjabbawockeez and helped organize HackTX with Freetail Hackers. I was also a a co-host of the Talking Robotics seminar series.

I’m currently a Production Engineer at Meta learning how to build secure, performant and reliable infrastructure for platforms that serve billions of people daily.
